About Me

The Scholar Behind the Work

I am an academically oriented law graduate with a strong commitment to legal scholarship, research, and higher legal education. My work demonstrates sustained engagement in doctrinal and socio-legal research, supported by leadership in law-centered academic and co-curricular initiatives.
